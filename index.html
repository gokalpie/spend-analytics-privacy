Privacy Policy

Spend Analytics does not collect or store any personal information on external servers. All personal data such as expenses, user-defined categories, and names are stored locally on the user's device using Core Data and are not shared with the developer.

However, Spend Analytics uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. This includes:

  • Google AdMob (for displaying ads)

These services may collect data such as your device type, IP address, advertising ID (IDFA), and interaction with advertisements, in order to serve personalized or contextual ads.

Data Collection Summary:

  • No user account is required.
  • All user-entered data is stored only on the device.
  • No analytics or tracking SDK is used, except for ad-related services.
